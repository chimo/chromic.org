ingredients:
- 2 oz gin
- 4-5 oz tonic water
- "Garnish: lime wedge"

directions:
- In a highball glass filled with ice cubes, pour the gin, then top with tonic.
- Gently stir so as to combine, but not so much so that you lose carbonation.
- Garnish with a lime wedge.
