ingredients:
- 3 oz RumChata Cream Liqueur
- 1 oz limoncello

directions:
- In a cocktail shaker filled with ice, pour the RumChata and limoncello
- Shake well
- Strain into an old-fashioned glass filled with fresh ice
- Serve
